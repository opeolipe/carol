Security is often treated as a technical checkbox. A firewall here, an encrypted database there. But for a Product Manager, security is a **trust relationship**. 

In my transition from traditional product strategy to cybersecurity, I've realized that the biggest vulnerability isn't always a bug in the code—it's a breakdown in the user's understanding of how their data is being used.

## The Trust Gap

When we build products at scale, we often optimize for "frictionless" experiences. However, when it comes to privacy, friction can actually be a feature. 

### Why Choice Matters
- Explicit consent vs. dark patterns.
- Clear data retention policies.
- Locally processed data (Edge computing).
